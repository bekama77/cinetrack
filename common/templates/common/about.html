About CineTrack

CineTrack is a Django application for building a personal movie journal. Users can maintain watchlists, create collections, and publish reviews for the community.

Track

Mark titles as watched, watching, or want to watch.

Review

Rate movies and write public reviews with owner-based permissions.

Discover

Get recommendations based on favorite genres saved in your profile.
